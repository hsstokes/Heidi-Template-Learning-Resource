Task 2: Create a Research Page

Use your digital skills from Task 1 to create an A3 page. Include both primary and secondary research with Harvard references.

Primary Research

Your own work

  • Your Photographs
  • Your Sketches
  • Your Collages
  • Your Samples

Secondary Research

Other sources

  • Artists and designers
  • Books/Articles and websites
  • Historical references

Steps

1 Collect

Gather your primary and secondary sources

2 Apply Techniques

Use your digital skills from Task 1

3 Arrange

Layout your page - print and add to physically if needed

4 Reference

Add Harvard references for all sources

Harvard Referencing

Reference all your secondary sources. For your own work, label it clearly.

For images:

Surname, Initial. (Year) Title [Medium]

For your own work:

Own photograph / Own sketch (Date, Location)
