Task 2: Digitally Enhance Your A3 Design Sheets

Scan your physical A3 sheets and apply Task 1 skills digitally to your design sheet.

Your Research Page Should Include

Primary Research

Your own work

  • Your Drawings/illustrations

Secondary Research

Other sources

  • Artists and designers

Steps

1 Scan

Scan or photograph your physical A3 research page clearly

3 Edit

Apply techniques from Task 1 - edit and integrate into an A3 sheet

4 Export

Save as JPEG or PDF
