What You Will Do

Scan your A3 sheet. Use Task 1 skills to improve it digitally.

Steps

1 Scan

Scan or photograph your A3 page clearly.

2 Open

Open in Photoshop.

  • Size: A3 Landscape
  • Resolution: 300 dpi
  • Colour: CMYK
3 Edit

Use your Task 1 techniques to improve the page.

4 Save

Export as JPEG or PDF.

Primary Research

Your own work:

  • Your photographs
  • Your drawings
  • Your collages
  • Your 3D samples

Secondary Research

Other sources:

  • Artists and designers
  • Books and websites
  • Historical references

Techniques to Try

  • Adjust brightness and contrast
  • Use levels to fix light scans
  • Use blending modes to soften images
  • Add text and information
  • Layer extra images over your work

Harvard Referencing

Add Harvard references for all secondary sources.

Example: Smith, J. (2020) Design Basics. London: Thames Press.
