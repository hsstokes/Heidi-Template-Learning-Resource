Task 2: Digitally Enhance Your A3 Design Sheets

Scan your physical A3 sheets and apply Task 1 skills digitally to your design sheet.

Your Research Page Should Include

Primary Research

Your own work

  • Your Photographs
  • Your Drawings/illustrations
  • Your Collages
  • Your 3D Samples

Secondary Research

Other sources

  • Artists and designers
  • Books, articles and websites
  • Historical references

Steps

1 Scan

Scan or photograph your physical A3 research page clearly

2 Open

Open scanned image in Photoshop: A3 Landscape 300res CMYK

3 Edit

Apply techniques from Task 1 - edit and integrate into an A3 sheet

4 Export

Save as JPEG or PDF

Techniques to Try

  • Adjust brightness and contrast to make images clearer
  • Use levels to fix washed-out scans
  • Use blending modes to soften blocky images
  • Make sure to add relevant digital text: referencing/ knowledge based information
  • Layer additional images/primary/secondary over your physical work

Harvard Referencing

Make sure your page includes Harvard references for all secondary sources.
